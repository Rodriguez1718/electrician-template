# GFCI Outlet Installation & Electrical Safety

Protect your family from electrical shock with professional GFCI outlet installation from VoltBully Electrical. Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter outlets are essential safety devices that can save lives by detecting dangerous electrical conditions and shutting off power instantly.

## What are GFCI Outlets?

### How GFCI Works
- **Ground Fault Detection**: Monitors electrical current flow and detects imbalances that indicate ground faults
- **Instant Protection**: Shuts off power in as little as 1/40th of a second when a ground fault is detected
- **Shock Prevention**: Prevents electrical shock that could cause injury or death
- **Fire Prevention**: Reduces risk of electrical fires caused by ground fault conditions

### GFCI vs Standard Outlets
- **Safety Features**: GFCI outlets have built-in safety monitoring that standard outlets lack
- **Test/Reset Buttons**: GFCI outlets include test and reset buttons for monthly safety testing
- **Code Requirements**: Required by electrical code in wet locations and high-risk areas
- **Downstream Protection**: Can protect multiple standard outlets on the same circuit

## Required GFCI Locations

### Indoor Locations
- **Bathrooms**: All outlets within 6 feet of sinks and bathtubs
- **Kitchens**: Countertop outlets and those within 6 feet of sinks
- **Laundry Rooms**: Outlets within 6 feet of laundry sinks
- **Garages**: All outlets in attached and detached garages
- **Basements**: Unfinished basement outlets and those near water sources
- **Crawl Spaces**: All outlets in crawl spaces at or below grade level

### Outdoor Locations
- **Exterior Outlets**: All outdoor outlets require GFCI protection
- **Pool Areas**: All outlets within 20 feet of swimming pools and spas
- **Hot Tubs**: Dedicated GFCI protection for hot tub electrical connections
- **Outdoor Kitchens**: All outlets in outdoor cooking and entertainment areas
- **Boat Docks**: Marine-grade GFCI protection for dock electrical systems
- **Landscape Lighting**: GFCI protection for outdoor lighting circuits

## Types of GFCI Protection

### GFCI Outlets
- **Standard GFCI**: Basic GFCI outlets with test/reset buttons
- **Weather Resistant**: Outdoor-rated GFCI outlets for exterior use
- **Tamper Resistant**: Child-safe GFCI outlets with built-in safety shutters
- **USB GFCI**: GFCI outlets with built-in USB charging ports

### GFCI Breakers
- **Panel Protection**: GFCI circuit breakers that protect entire circuits
- **Multiple Outlet Protection**: Single breaker protecting multiple outlets
- **Dedicated Circuits**: GFCI breakers for appliances and equipment
- **Retrofit Solutions**: GFCI breakers for older homes without GFCI outlets

### Portable GFCI
- **Temporary Protection**: Portable GFCI devices for construction and outdoor use
- **Extension Cords**: GFCI-protected extension cords for temporary power
- **Adapters**: Plug-in GFCI adapters for existing outlets
- **Power Strips**: GFCI-protected power strips for multiple devices
